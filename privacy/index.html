Lysto Privacy Policy

Last Updated:

Lysto ("we", "our", or "the app") values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is collected, used, and shared when you use our application.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information when you use the app:

a. Personal Information

When you create an account or log in (e.g., using Sign in with Apple), we may collect the following:

  • Email address
  • First and Last Name (if shared)
  • User ID

b. Usage and Device Information

We may automatically collect certain data to improve our service:

  • Device model and operating system version (iOS/Android)
  • IP address
  • App usage statistics and performance data
  • Language preferences

c. Advertising Data (AdMob)

Our app uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may use technologies such as device identifiers (IDFA, AAID) and cookies to provide you with personalized ads.

2. How We Use Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide the core features of the app (creating shopping lists, setting up groups).
  • To manage your account and verify your identity.
  • To send you app-related notifications (Push Notifications).
  • To improve the app experience and fix bugs.
  • To show relevant advertisements via Google AdMob.

3. Third-Party Services

This app uses third-party service providers to process data or provide additional features:

  • Supabase: Used for database management and authentication services. Your data is stored on secure servers.
  • Google AdMob: Provides ad display within the app. For more information on how Google uses data, please visit the Google Privacy & Terms page.
  • Apple Sign-In: Used for secure login on iOS devices.

4. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures (encryption, secure servers) to protect your personal data. However, please note that no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

5. User Rights and Account Deletion

You can permanently delete your account and all associated data (lists, groups, etc.) using the "Delete my account" option located in the "Settings" menu within the app. You also have the right to access, correct, or request the deletion of your personal data.

6. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make any changes, we will post the new policy on this page.
